Overview :
-This game name’s is Build and Shoot .This project is a mini game which is created on unity engine. This is a game on mini games web pages, so everybody can play online just for fun or if they like the game too much they can download it on the webpage to their desktop in order to enjoy the game. The project have a simple game play. Players can control his or her character, this character can use bricks to create the path that force or allow zombies to move as his way. I’m using unity to create this game, because it is easy to export and require only c-sharp. The special about unity is its adaptation to different computer’s configuration, so the stronger configuration is the better performance that unity can delivery to the users.

.Motivation :
Motivation : -My game is attractive because of its simplicity. The gameplay is easy for the players to understand the game flow, so people can feel they are focusing on the game mission. Because the game is created on Unity, therefore it has an flexible option in selecting an appropriate configuration that is suitable for the computer’s specification. It also does not require complicated skills to create the project, only c-sharp programming and basic logical algorithm are necessary, so the final product is high quality.

Description :

-High speed: Build and shoot is a highspeed action game. The speed factor can boost the energy of the players, therefore they will have an extremely excited experience when they interact with the character in the game. I also add in heavy metal music to make the players be more aggressive , and react faster against the enemy attacks. This factor is special effect to make them feel like they are warriors who having a battle bad force. The impact of pushing the mentality of the players is a first step to attract them, so they are willing to take more challenges and move to the next level. Eventually they are going to push themselves to the limit in order to reach to the highest level.

-Creativity :This project is not only has a rushing gameplay, it also provide a shopping place for the players after they defeat a level. The shop have different types of weapon and brick, each one of them have unique effect in order to make players to create their own playstyle. For example shotgun is a short range weapon, so it will be a good combination with big brick because big brick has a small quantity but has a high duration. Duration in build and shoot is factor that measure how well the brick can hold against bullet and zombie attacks, therefore it can last long for the shotgun to wipe out all zombie at that level.

-High risk high reward: My mini-game has a melee weapon that can deal a big amount of damage in the range of 70 degree in front of them character. If the zombies are destroyed by melee weapon it would drop more money and more capacity of supplement such as heath, armor , pieces of brick. But the downside can put the players into dangerous situation, because melee weapons require the character to put him/herself into a range that both zombies and players can attack. At that extremely close range numerous zombies will attack simultaneously, so the result maybe a major injure or can be an instant death.

-Highly competitive: Even though build and shoot is a mini-game but it has a scoreboard for the players to show the others their achievement. But they are only allowed to save their score on the specific scoreboard, it means there are 3 of them which are easy medium and hard. So players can only save base on the mod that they selected. This maybe make players feel sad sometimes, but it keep them to try and improve more. Especially users can see the score in other mods to encourage them to try their best.. There are only 30 levels but the ways to increase the score are variable such as using melee weapons, shoot into the head of the zombie, pass a level without taking any damage. These tips may enhance the skills of each player, so may join the higher position in the competition.

-Interesting story line: Most of mini-games do not have a story because developer try to make it as simple as possible. But in my opinion a game without a story is incomplete, users will not able to understand what is happening. There are players do not care about the story because with them action is matter, how about the others. That is why build and shoot have a conversation of the character about each the place he or she is going to fight, so the curiosity of players will make the game more interesting. I also make a skip button for who refer only action instead of reading a story line to satisfy their requirements.
